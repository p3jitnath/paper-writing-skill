# Introduction Checklist

- Does the opening identify a specific Earth-system problem, scale, and consequence?
- Is the gap scientific, methodological, computational, or operational rather than generic “low accuracy”?
- Does the core insight precede the model name?
- Are contributions written as findings with evidence boundaries?
- Does each contribution map to a result or diagnostic?
- Does the preview name the reference, metric, domain, period/lead time, and uncertainty needed to interpret the headline result?
- Are physical, causal, generalization, and operational claims no stronger than the evidence?
